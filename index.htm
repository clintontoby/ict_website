ICT UNIT

The ICT (Information and Communication Technology) unit in our university manages and supports the technological infrastructure, including networks, servers, and software. They ensure the availability and functionality of IT systems, provide technical support to students and staff, manage digital resources, and implement security measures for data and information. Additionally, the ICT unit often plays a role in planning and implementing technology-related projects to enhance the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the university's operations.

network engineer/system administrator holds the responsibility of managing and maintaining the entire network infrastructure and computer systems to ensure seamless and secure operations. Their tasks include designing, implementing, and overseeing the organization's computer networks, including local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and internet connections. They are responsible for installing and configuring network equipment, such as routers, switches, and firewalls, and ensuring that these systems operate efficiently. Network engineers/system administrators manage user accounts, permissions, and access rights to various educational resources and platforms. They troubleshoot network and system issues, conduct routine maintenance, and implement security measures to protect sensitive data.

SERVER

server is a computer or system that is designed to manage and facilitate network resources and provide services to other computers, referred to as clients, within a network.

INVERTER & BATTERY

an inverter is a device used to convert direct current (DC) power into alternating current (AC) power. This conversion is necessary because many data center components and equipment, including servers, typically operate on AC power. Inverters ensure that the DC power from backup sources, such as batteries or generators, can be converted to AC power to maintain continuous operation during power outages or fluctuations.

COOLING SYSTEM

cooling system in the context of computers and servers is designed to regulate and maintain the temperature within electronic devices to prevent overheating.
